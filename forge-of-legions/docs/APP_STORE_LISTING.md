## App Description

### Short Description (80 characters)
Epic turn-based tactical battles inspired by tabletop miniature wargaming!

### Full Description

**Command legendary armies. Paint your warriors. Conquer the realm!**

Forge of Legions brings the beloved experience of tabletop miniature wargaming to your pocket. Whether you're a veteran of the hobby or completely new to tactical warfare, this game offers deep strategy with easy-to-learn mechanics.

**BUILD YOUR ARMY**
Choose from three unique factions, each with distinct playstyles:
- The Golden Empire: Disciplined human forces with powerful formations
- The Savage Horde: Brutal orc warriors that overwhelm with raw power
- The Risen Dead: Undying legions that grow stronger as battle progresses

**PAINT YOUR UNITS**
Just like real miniature wargaming, customize every unit in your army! Pick colors for armor, cloth, weapons, and more. Create unique color schemes that represent YOUR legion on the battlefield.

**TACTICAL DEPTH**
Master turn-based combat with:
- Movement and positioning strategy
- Unique unit abilities and synergies
- Terrain advantages and objectives
- Critical hits and counterattacks

**CAMPAIGN MODE**
Experience "The Shattered Realms" - an epic story campaign with:
- 7+ challenging missions
- Progressive difficulty
- Unlock new units and factions
- Earn gold and experience

**MULTIPLAYER BATTLES**
Challenge friends or random opponents:
- Create private games with shareable codes
- Quick match for instant battles
- Climb the global leaderboard
- Prove you're the ultimate commander

**EASY TO LEARN**
New to wargaming? Our interactive tutorial teaches you:
- Unit statistics and combat
- Movement and attack ranges
- Special abilities and tactics
- Army composition strategies

**FEATURES AT A GLANCE**
- Turn-based tactical combat
- 15+ unique unit types
- 3 playable factions
- Unit painting/customization
- Army builder with point costs
- Story campaign mode
- PvP multiplayer
- Daily rewards system
- Works offline

Perfect for fans of tabletop wargaming, chess, and tactical RPGs. Play a quick skirmish during a painting break or dive into an epic campaign!

**Download now and forge your legacy!**
