# Forge of Legions - Competitive Pricing Strategy

## Executive Summary

Forge of Legions targets the intersection of mobile gaming and the tabletop wargaming community. Our pricing strategy balances accessibility for casual players with meaningful purchases for enthusiasts, while avoiding predatory pay-to-win mechanics that alienate the core tabletop gaming audience.

---

## Market Analysis

### Competitor Pricing

| Game | Model | Price Range | Notes |
|------|-------|-------------|-------|
| Warhammer 40K: Tacticus | Freemium | $0-$100+ | Heavy gacha, pay-to-win concerns |
| Warhammer Quest | Premium | $4.99-$6.99 | One-time purchase + IAPs |
| Total War Battles | Freemium | $0-$50+ | Energy system, character packs |
| Fire Emblem Heroes | Freemium | $0-$200+ | Gacha-heavy, controversial |
| Chess.com | Freemium | $0-$8.33/mo | Subscription model |
| Polytopia | Premium | $0.99-$3.99 | Base free, faction purchases |

### Target Market Insights

**Warhammer Fans (Primary Target)**
- Already spend $50-500+ annually on the hobby
- Value quality over quantity
- Skeptical of pay-to-win mobile games
- Appreciate customization and ownership

**Strategy Gamers (Secondary Target)**
- Prefer skill-based competition
- Will pay for ad-free experiences
- Value fair multiplayer

---

## Recommended Pricing Model: "Fair Freemium"

### Core Philosophy
- **Free to Play**: Full game accessible without payment
- **No Pay-to-Win**: All gameplay content earnable through play
- **Cosmetic Focus**: Premium purchases for customization
- **Respect Player Time**: Reasonable progression without artificial gates

---

## Monetization Tiers

### Tier 1: Free Players (70% of users)

**What's Included:**
- Complete single-player campaign
- All core units (3 per faction, 9 total)
- Basic paint colors (15 colors)
- Unlimited skirmish battles
- Multiplayer access
- Daily rewards system

**Progression:**
- Earn 50-200 gold per battle
- Campaign rewards unlock content
- Daily login bonuses (up to 500 gold/week)

### Tier 2: Supporters ($1-5 purchases, 20% of users)

**Starter Pack - $4.99** (Best Value - One-time offer)
- 1000 Gold
- 50 Gems
- Exclusive cosmetic
- Removes ads for 30 days
- Conversion target: First 3 days

**Gold Pouch - $1.99**
- 500 Gold
- Equivalent to 5-10 battles
- For players who want faster unit unlocks

**Gem Bundle - $2.99**
- 100 Gems
- Premium paint colors
- Special effects

### Tier 3: Enthusiasts ($10-25 purchases, 8% of users)

**Commander Pack - $9.99**
- 3000 Gold
- 150 Gems
- One faction fully unlocked
- Exclusive commander unit
- Permanent ad removal

**Season Pass - $7.99/month** (Future)
- All new units immediately
- Exclusive seasonal cosmetics
- Bonus daily rewards
- Priority matchmaking

### Tier 4: Collectors ($25+ purchases, 2% of users)

**Ultimate Bundle - $19.99**
- All units unlocked
- All paint colors
- 10000 Gold + 500 Gems
- Exclusive effects
- "Founder" badge

**Faction Packs - $6.99 each**
- Complete faction unlock
- Faction-specific cosmetics
- For targeted purchases

## Pricing Psychology

### Price Points Rationale

| Price | Psychology | Purpose |
|-------|------------|---------|
| $1.99 | Impulse purchase threshold | Entry point, test buyer |
| $4.99 | "Cup of coffee" | Main conversion target |
| $9.99 | Single digit comfort | Serious player investment |
| $19.99 | Premium but not excessive | Collectors, whales |

### Discounts & Promotions

**Launch Promotion (First 2 weeks)**
- Starter Pack: $2.99 (40% off)
- Commander Pack: $6.99 (30% off)

**Seasonal Sales**
- Holiday sales: 25-50% off bundles
- New content launches: Introductory pricing

**Loyalty Rewards**
- Returning player bonuses
- Consecutive purchase discounts

4. Launch discount promotion

### Phase 2: Expansion (Months 4-6)
1. Add faction packs
2. Introduce seasonal cosmetics
3. Commander Pack bundle

## Ethical Considerations

### What We DON'T Do
- No loot boxes or gacha
- No pay-to-win advantages
- No artificial energy limits
- No predatory pricing for children
- No dark patterns in UI

### What We DO
- Clear pricing information
- Earn everything through play
- Cosmetic-only premium content
- Fair multiplayer matching
- Parental controls option

---

## Conclusion

Forge of Legions' "Fair Freemium" model prioritizes long-term player trust and community building over short-term revenue extraction. By respecting the tabletop gaming audience's values and avoiding predatory practices, we aim to build a sustainable, growing player base that advocates for the game organically.

**Recommended Initial Pricing:**
- Base Game: **FREE**
- Starter Pack: **$4.99** (launch: $2.99)
- Small Purchases: **$1.99-$2.99**
- Premium Bundles: **$9.99-$19.99**

This strategy positions Forge of Legions as the ethical alternative in mobile wargaming, appealing to players frustrated with exploitative monetization in the genre.
